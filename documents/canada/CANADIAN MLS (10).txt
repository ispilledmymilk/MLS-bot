6. Prairie Provinces (Manitoba & Saskatchewan)


6.1 WinnipegREALTORS / Manitoba
Official WinnipegREALTORS website: winnipegrealtors.ca
Winnipeg Regional Real Estate Board: winnipegregionalrealestateboard.ca


* DDF Participation: Manitoba boards participate in CREA DDF, feeding data into the national pool.
* Local IDX: WinnipegREALTORS provides IDX feeds to members; a signed IDX agreement is required.
* Regulatory Reform: Manitoba replaced the Real Estate Brokers Act (REBA, from 1947) with the new Real Estate Services Act and Regulation in 2022.


6.2 Saskatchewan REALTORS Association (SRA)
Official SRA website: saskatchewanrealtorsassociation.ca
SRA MLS System page: saskatchewanrealtorsassociation.ca/mls-system


Saskatchewan does NOT participate in the CREA DDF national feed. This is a critical distinction — agents needing Saskatchewan listings must access the SRA regional MLS directly.
	

* Direct SRA Access Required: No national feed alternative exists for Saskatchewan.
* Sold Listings: Saskatchewan IDX/reciprocity does NOT permit display of sold listings or co-mingling of listings from other boards.
* Minimum Listing Duration: SRA rules require a minimum listing duration of 60 days on the MLS System.
* PST: A 6% Saskatchewan Provincial Sales Tax (PST) applies to IDX set-up and monthly/annual fees.
