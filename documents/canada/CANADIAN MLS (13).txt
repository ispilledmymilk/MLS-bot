7. Atlantic Provinces


7.1 Nova Scotia — NSAR
Official NSAR website: nsrealtors.ca
Nova Scotia Real Estate Commission (NSREC — regulator): nsrec.ns.ca


* Membership: NSAR represents 2,000+ brokers and salespeople across the province. MLS access is exclusive to NSAR members.
* Paragon Platform: The provincial MLS platform is Paragon (since 2003). Members list and search province-wide.
* Accessibility Fields (2024): NSAR became the first MLS System in Canada to make accessibility measurement fields (door widths, hallway widths, turning radii) mandatory as of June 2024.
* AI Compliance Automation (2025): NSAR partnered with a tech firm to automate MLS compliance checking using AI, handling 8,000+ listings efficiently.
* DDF Participation: Nova Scotia participates in CREA DDF.


7.2 New Brunswick — NBREA
Official NBREA website: nbrea.ca


* DDF Participation: NB boards participate in CREA DDF.
* IDX/VOW: Standard IDX and VOW feeds available to licensed NBREA members.


7.3 Newfoundland & Labrador — NLREA
Official NLREA website: nlrea.ca


* DDF Participation: NLREA participates in CREA DDF.
* Local IDX: Available to licensed members; direct contact with NLREA required for feed agreements.


7.4 Prince Edward Island — PEIARA
Official PEIARA website: peiara.ca


* DDF Participation: PEIARA participates in CREA DDF.
* IDX Access: Available to licensed members; agreements must be signed with PEIARA.
