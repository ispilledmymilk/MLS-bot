9. Universal Compliance Checklist


9.1 Access & Membership
* Only licensed CREA members in good standing may access DDF or regional MLS data feeds.
* Non-members are strictly prohibited from accessing any Canadian MLS feed or scraping REALTOR.ca.
* A signed IDX/VOW data agreement must be in place with your board.


9.2 Attribution & Display
* The listing brokerage must always be identified on property detail pages (on first view).
* CREA trademarks (MLS, REALTOR) must be displayed correctly and may not be modified.
* The applicable board/CREA mandatory footer disclaimer must appear on every page displaying MLS data.


9.3 Photos & Media
* All watermarks on listing photos must be preserved. No cropping, blurring, or overlaying of watermarks.
* AI-enhanced or virtually staged photos require disclosure and must be accompanied by the original unstaged version.
* Stock photos of nearby amenities are not permitted unless the amenity is directly visible from the property.


9.4 Purge & Data Freshness
* Expired, Withdrawn, or Terminated listings must be removed from your public site within 24 hours of status change.
* Active listing data must be refreshed at least every 24 hours via scheduled API/RESO updates.


9.5 VOW Requirements
* Registration wall: Full name and valid email required before access to sold/expired data.
* Email verification: Required before granting VOW access.
* Audit logs: User identity and listing view records must be maintained for at least 1 year (TRREB) or 180 days post-password expiry (GVR/BC).
* No search engine indexing: noindex/nofollow required on all VOW-gated pages.


9.6 Prohibited Activities
* No data mining, scraping, or bulk copying of MLS data.
* No redistribution of MLS data to unauthorized third parties.
* No co-mingling of listings from different boards without clear per-listing source labeling.
* No use of BC Assessment data or equivalent provincial assessment data for solicitation or harassment.
