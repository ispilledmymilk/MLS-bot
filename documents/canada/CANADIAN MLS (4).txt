3. British Columbia


3.1 Greater Vancouver REALTORS (GVR / formerly REBGV)
Official GVR website: gvrealtors.ca
GVR Member portal & WebAPI/BridgeAPI transition FAQ: member.gvrealtors.ca
GVR IDX compliance inquiries: idx@gvrealtors.ca (email)
BridgeAPI platform (WebAPI feed): bridgeinteractive.com


3.1.1 Access Requirements
* Membership: You must be a licensed REALTOR with GVR, CADREB (Chilliwack), or FVREB (Fraser Valley) to access these feeds.
* Reciprocal Agreement: GVR, FVREB, and CADREB share data reciprocally — one connection provides data from all three boards.
* RESO Web API (BridgeAPI): RETS has been fully deprecated (deadline: February 28, 2025). ALL integrations must now use the RESO Web API via the BridgeAPI platform.
* Compliance Review: After development, your website must be submitted for compliance review by GVR's IDX department. Production credentials are not issued until compliance is confirmed.


3.1.2 IDX Display Rules
* Members may only advertise authorized prices and terms.
* Sold prices cannot be advertised before public registry availability or without buyer and seller consent.
* SOLD signs require seller permission and compliance with local board rules.
* Public representations must be factual and include disclaimers for board-sourced information.


3.1.3 VOW Rules
* Registrants must acknowledge privacy notices, non-commercial use restrictions, and data redistribution prohibitions upon VOW access.
* Registrants must agree not to copy, redistribute, or engage in data mining.
* VOW participants must maintain user records for at least 180 days after password expiration.
* BC Assessment Data: Members are prohibited from using BC Assessment data for solicitation or any form of harassment.
