2. Agent License Verification — JECVEBR
Before granting any agent or agency access to publish listings on the platform, verify that they hold a valid, active JECVEBR license and a current $10,000 surety bond. This is the platform's onboarding gate — the license is the minimum legal credential to mediate property transactions in Puerto Rico.
