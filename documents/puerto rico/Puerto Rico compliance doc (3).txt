Puerto Rico's MLS is operated by Stellar MLS in partnership with PRAR. The platform's IDX and VOW access comes through this relationship. All Stellar MLS rules that apply to Florida listings apply identically to Puerto Rico listings. The MLS platform is Matrix. Feed contact: PRAR@stellarmls.com / 800-686-7451 option 4.

3.2 VOW Rules
* Registration wall: full legal name + valid email address required before accessing VOW data.
* Email verification: confirmation email sent and verified before access granted — no exceptions.
* Terms of Use: consumer must affirmatively accept TOU restricting data to personal non-commercial use.
* Redistribution: VOW data may not be redistributed or retransmitted by the consumer.
* Audit logs: maintain logs of all VOW registrations and listings viewed per user. Required for Stellar MLS compliance reviews.
* Sold price data: Puerto Rico is a full disclosure jurisdiction — sold prices available via VOW without restriction. No non-disclosure flag applies to any PR listing.


3.3 Clear Cooperation Policy — Puerto Rico Specific Rule
PR-SPECIFIC WINDOW: Stellar MLS applies NAR's Clear Cooperation Policy 8.0 to Puerto Rico with a five (5) business day entry window — not the standard one business day. An agent has 5 business days from signing a listing agreement to enter the listing into Matrix, OR file a Temporary Exclusion or Office Exclusive form. If any public marketing occurs during this window, CCP is triggered and the listing must be entered into the MLS within one business day of that marketing event.


Public marketing triggers — any of the following activates the one business day MLS entry requirement:
* Yard signs or any physical marketing materials
* Any digital marketing on a public website, including social media posts
* Emails sent to more than one outside brokerage
* One-to-one broker communications do NOT trigger CCP


3.4 Zillow Listing Access Standards — Applies to Puerto Rico
* Zillow's LAS requires any publicly marketed property to appear in Stellar MLS within one business day to be eligible for Zillow display.
* If a Puerto Rico listing is publicly marketed for more than 24 hours before MLS entry, Zillow may ban that listing for the full duration of the listing agreement.
* Zillow Preview (announced March 17, 2026 — live April 2026): pre-market listings publicly displayed on Zillow/Trulia before MLS entry. Will include Puerto Rico listings. Platforms must handle the new Preview listing status type in feed ingestion logic.
* Platform obligation: surface LAS compliance status for all PR listings. Flag any listing that has been publicly marketed without corresponding Stellar MLS entry.


3.5 DMEL — Delayed Marketing Exempt Listing
Stellar MLS implemented the DMEL category on September 16, 2025, applying identically to Florida and Puerto Rico.
* Sellers must sign the Owners Exclusion Form before DMEL status can be applied.
* The signed Owners Exclusion Form must be uploaded within 24 hours of any MLS request.
* During the delay period: listing visible to MLS participants and registered VOW clients only. Does NOT appear on public IDX portals.
* After delay period: immediately eligible for full IDX and VOW distribution.

3.7 New Member Training Gate
* All new Stellar MLS participants must complete required training within 60 days of joining.
* Platform obligation: agents who have not completed training CANNOT add or edit listings in Matrix. Do not accept listing submissions from agents flagged as training-incomplete.
* Training available in both English and Spanish.
