10. Antitrust and Competition Law — 2026
10.1 FTC / State AG Action Against Zillow and Redfin — Corrected
The FTC filed suit on September 30, 2025, against Zillow and Redfin centering on a multifamily rental syndication agreement. The complaint alleges the $100 million deal effectively eliminated Redfin as a competitor in the ILS advertising market for large unit complexes.
IMPORTANT CORRECTION — MISSING CO-PLAINTIFFS: Five state Attorneys General co-filed identical allegations on October 1, 2025: Arizona, Connecticut, New York, Virginia, and Washington State. The cases were consolidated. All compliance JSON files referencing this litigation must include all six plaintiffs (FTC + 5 state AGs) and note venue as the Eastern District of Virginia.


10.2 Compass v. Zillow — Corrected Case Status
CRITICAL LEGAL CORRECTION: The February 6, 2026 ruling in Compass v. Zillow ONLY denied Compass's motion for a preliminary injunction. This is NOT a final judgment. The court found Compass had not shown a likelihood of success on the merits at the injunction stage. The underlying antitrust case is still ACTIVE. Do not characterize this ruling as confirming the legality of Zillow's LAS policy.
